Quickwrite

What if you could make a blog...out of your gists on Github?


Introducing Quickwrite!

Quickwrite links to your github account to pull your gists. Once it does so, it renders them with fun style.

Not interested in writing on Github? Quickwrite provides its own text editor with a markdown renderer so that you can build your blog entirely on Quickwrite. It'll just use gists as a database in the background.
